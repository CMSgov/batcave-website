Open Source Behind the BATcave

The team behind the BATcave is passionate about open source software and making the world a safer place through public and transparent commitments. We've built our systems leveraging open source software and want to ensure we pay it back and forward for government agencies of any kind to advance their continuous security, rapid software development, and streamlined ATO efforts.

For more information on how you can be involved, check out our contributor guide, fork a repository, say hi at batcave@cms.hhs.gov.
